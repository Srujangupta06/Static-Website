What Is Organic Farming?

Natural farming is an ecological approach that focuses on maintaining soil health, promoting biodiversity, and minimizing human intervention by relying on natural processes rather than synthetic inputs. It seeks to create a self-sustaining agricultural system by working harmoniously with nature.
